WatchDog is a **defensive security tool** designed for authorized use only.

## Authorized Use

This tool is intended for:

- **System administrators** monitoring their own infrastructure
- **Security teams** detecting unauthorized access to their systems
- **Home users** protecting their personal devices
- **Authorized security testing** with explicit written permission

## Prohibited Use

This tool must NOT be used for:

- Deploying on systems you do not own or have authorization to monitor
- Any unauthorized access to computer systems
- Any illegal activity
- Offensive operations or attacks
